## Brainwave
A workshop exploring autonomous compute.

We started Brainwave because we wanted to build things we believe in, with people we trust, at a pace that lets us get it right.

We work at the intersection of hardware and software:
- Infrastructure for real-time perception
- Efficient inference for multimodal models
- Foundations for heterogeneous compute

We think clarity and a commitment to craft are non-negotiable.

We're not in a hurry.
